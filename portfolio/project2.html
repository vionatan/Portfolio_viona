Banner Design: Loving Enviroment

Overview

This banner design was created to bring awareness as to how people constantly care less about littering the ground and disposing trash improperly by showing the contrast between the horrible environment and a beautiful greenery environment. It also shows how we often take pictures of beautiful scenery, storing it in our phone while we neglect the ugly side of the environment, for instance people dumping food boxes and bags of trash on the grass after cooking near the barbeque pit. But overall, the artwork allows people to know that they also have the power to protect our loving environment.
