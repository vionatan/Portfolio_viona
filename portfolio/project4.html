Overview

The Crowding app was a team-based project dedicated to show users nearby accomodations at their current location and crowded is it based on safe-entries made. This was made so they would not have to wait in long queues and go to crowded places during the COVID period. On the other hand they will be advised to other places with lesser crowd.
